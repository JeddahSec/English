* Dialogue

| Teacher | Who do you live with, Nina?                                                                                                                             |
| Nina    | I live with my mother, my father and my grandmother.                                                                                                    |
| Teacher | What is your mother like?                                                                                                                               |
| Nina    | My mother is kind and hard-working. She is a doctor, so she works really long hours.                                                                    |
| Teacher | What do you and your mother like doing together?                                                                                                        |
| Nina    | When my mother isn't working, we like watching TV together. We also enjoy listening to music. She really likes gardening, but I don't like that at all. |
| Teacher | What is your grandmother like?                                                                                                                          |
| Nina    | She is similar to my father. They are both sociable and active.                                                                                         |
| Teacher | What do you all like doing together?                                                                                                                    |
| Nina    | We love cooking, eating, and watching TV together.                                                                                                      |
