* Dialogue
| Teacher | Why are you learning English, Haruto?                                                                                                                                                      |
| Haruto  | I'm learning English to meet new people. Also, to make my parents happy!                                                                                                                   |
| Teacher | What do you like about learning English?                                                                                                                                                   |
| Haruto  | Well, I like speaking English. I also like practicing pronunciation. But there are some things I don't like. I don't like learning English grammar at all. It's very boring and difficult. |
| Teacher | What else do you find difficult about English?                                                                                                                                             |
| Haruto  | I think learning English vocabulary is difficult and tiring. I think writing is difficult, too. And boring!                                                                                |
| Teacher | What do you find easy about learning English?                                                                                                                                              |
