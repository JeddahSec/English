* Dialogue
| Teacher  | Where do you live, Hyun-woo?                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Hyun-woo | I live in an apartment in Seoul, South Korea.                                                                                                                                                 |
| Teacher  | Where in Seoul is your apartment?                                                                                                                                                             |
| Hyun-woo | My apartment is in the city center.                                                                                                                                                           |
| Teacher  | What is your home like?                                                                                                                                                                       |
| Hyun-woo | It is small, but it's comfortable. It has one bedroom, a kitchen, a bathroom, and a living room. We have a nice balcony and a garage for our car.                                             |
| Teacher  | Is it a modern apartment, Hyun-woo?                                                                                                                                                           |
| Hyun-woo | No, it's quite old. But it's not cold or damp. How about you? Where do you live?                                                                                                              |
| Teacher  | I live in an old house in the countryside. It's small and dark. It has a kitchen, a living room, a bedroom and a bathroom. But it has a very large garden, and it's by a river, so I love it! |
